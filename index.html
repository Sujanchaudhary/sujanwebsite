    Rules and Regulations to be followed in college compound


    #Regarding Timing:
     1. No one can go outside the classroom without the permission of respective teacher.
    2. Students are not allowed to go to cafeteria and toilet during Grade hours. (If needed, only in emergency with permission of teacher.)
    #Regarding Uniform:
     1. Uniform is must.
    2. No student will be allowed to enter the school compound without proper uniform in school days
    3. Students are requested to wear descent dress.
    4. Students are allowed to wear out-dress only on their
    birthday.
    5. Students are strictly restricted to visit parties, caf√© and restaurants in school uniform.
    6. Unwanted ornaments are strictly prohibited.
    #Regarding Leave:
     1. Application should be submitted one day before leave to the Class teacher. (Doctor's prescription is must in case of being ill.)
    Rs. 100/- will be fined (each day) for being absent in classroom without any solid reason.
    2. Students who are absent for more than 15 consecutive days without information are subject to expulsion from the school.
    #Regarding Library:
     1. Library card is must to borrow books from the library.
    2. The book is to be returned within a week otherwise Rs. 2/- will be charged for each day.
    3. Students are directed not to lose or tear the books borrowed from the library, if so, they have to pay as much money as the original book does.
    4. Hue and cry won't be entertained in the library.
    #Regarding Games:
     1. ID card of the concerned student is required to take sports materials. 2. Sports materials must be submitted before warning bell otherwise concerned student will be deprived of borrowing sports materials. 3. Concerned student will be responsible for losing sports materials and will be fined accordingly.
    #Regarding Computer Lab:
     1. No operation of computer without permission.
    2. Computer setting and networking are not allowed to be operated. If so, concerned student will be penalized.
    3. Students are requested to turn off the computer after the use of it.
    #Regarding Monthly and Exam Fee:
     1. Monthly fee is to be paid before every 3rd day of the month otherwise Rs. 50/- will be fined each day according to school's rules.
    2. Written application will be entertained before the 2nd day of month in case of being late.
    3. Fine will be excluded after the submission of written application before the 2nd day of month. (only once in a year)
    4. Students have to pay Exam Fee for the Three Terminal Exams.
    5. Penalty fee Rs. 500/- should be paid if the student does not take part in the exam without any severe reason and Pre-permission.
